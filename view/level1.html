    Pilot

    A pilot is a professional who has skills in operating an airplane. Its main job is to carry aircraft, whether commercial, military, or private, from one location to another safely and efficiently.

    Nahkoda

    The Captain are responsible for the operation and navigation of the ship, as well as the safety and welfare of its crew and passengers. Their duties include route planning, ship security oversight, crew management, and handling emergency situations.

    Guru

    A teacher is an educator who plays an important role in guiding and teaching students at various levels of education, from preschool to college.

    Masinis

    A machinist is a professional who is responsible for the operation and maintenance of machines or mechanical equipment, such as train engines, airplanes, or other industrial machines.

    Farmer

    Farmers are individuals who are responsible for food production, be it crops or livestock, on a small or large scale.

    Pemadam Kebakaran

    Firefighters are professionals trained to handle fires and other related emergency situations. Their primary duties are responding to fire calls, extinguishing fires, rescuing victims, and protecting property from damage.

    Dokter

    A doctor is a medical professional responsible for the diagnosis, treatment, and care of patients who are sick or have a medical condition.

    Polisi

    The police are law enforcers whose job is to maintain order, prevent crime, and enforce the law in society. The main duties of a police officer include patrolling, investigating crimes, arresting criminals, and providing assistance in emergency.

    Artist

    An artist is an individual who creates works of art, whether in the form of paintings, sculptures, music, literature, or other forms of art.

    Astronaut

    An astronaut is a professional trained to travel into space. The main duties of an astronaut include flying a spaceship, carrying out scientific research missions, conducting experiments.

    Athlete

    An athlete is someone who is professionally involved in sports and frequently competes in various sporting events. The main task of an athlete is to train their body to have maximum performance in a particular sport, be it athletics, team, or individual.

    Chef

    A chef is a professional in the culinary field who has knowledge and skills in cooking and processing food.

    Dentist

    A dentist, or dentist, is a medical professional who specializes in dental and oral health care. The main duties of a dentist include routine dental examinations, cleaning teeth, treating tooth and gum disease, and carrying out restorative procedures such as filling teeth or installing braces.

    Flight Attendant

    A flight attendant or steward is an aviation professional who is responsible for the comfort, safety and needs of passengers during a flight.

    Judge

    A judge is a court official responsible for law enforcement and dispute resolution in the justice system. Their primary duties include listening to arguments from both sides in a trial, assessing the evidence presented, and rendering fair decisions.

    Scientist

    A scientist is a researcher who studies and investigates natural phenomena or phenomena in the laboratory.

    Soldier

    A soldier is a military professional dedicated to protecting his or her country and society from external and internal threats.
